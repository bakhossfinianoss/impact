The great benefit to purchasing a business owner’s policy in Quebec...

The great benefit to purchasing a business owner’s policy in Quebec, as opposed to purchasing each coverage individually, is that a BOP is typically sold for less premium than the total cost of each individual coverage lumped together...

Business owner policies will cover different perils and assets, varying from one policy to the next. The point of purchasing a comprehensive BOP is that it is specifically designed to cover the risks that your business may be exposed to, whether that’s due to its physical location, its industry, its operations, etc. Here are just a few of the items that your business owner policy insurance may include:

Commercial General Liability

CGL, or commercial general liability insurance, is an insurance policy designed to protect businesses from third-party liability claims alleging property damage or bodily injury as a result of your business’ operations or at a client’s site while your employees are doing work. This is the most integral form of coverage a business can purchase, as it offers basic liability coverage against claims that could otherwise put your company out of work for good.
