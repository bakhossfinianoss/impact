CAR Insurance

Every automobile owner in Quebec must have at least liability insurance to cover damages caused by your fault to another person. This is often referred to as “one-side” insurance (Chapter A). Optional private insurance (Chapter B) covers damages to your vehicle resulting from a collision for which you are responsible. It can also cover fire, theft, and vandalism.

MOTORCYCLE Insurance

Motorcycle insurance in Quebec is essential for motorcycle owners, offering protection against specific risks associated with riding motorcycles. Here are the key points to know:

Liability Insurance: Mandatory like for cars, it covers bodily injury and property damage caused to others in an accident for which you are responsible.

Collision Insurance: Also optional, it covers damages to your motorcycle resulting from a collision with another vehicle or a stationary object, regardless of who is at fault.

Uninsured or Underinsured Motorist Coverage: Protects against damages caused by drivers who are not adequately insured or not insured at all.

RECREATIONAL VEHICLE Insurance

Recreational vehicle insurance is a type of insurance that protects owners of recreational vehicles from different risks and potential damages related to their use. It usually covers the following:

Protection against Damages:
Vehicle Damage: Covers repairs if the vehicle is damaged in an accident, collision, or by events like storms, floods, or fires.
Theft: Protects against the theft of the vehicle or its equipment.

Liability Coverage:
Damage to Others: Covers costs if you cause injury or damage to other people or their property while using the recreational vehicle.
Personal Liability: Protects you from legal action if you are held responsible for an accident.

Roadside Assistance:
Breakdown Help: Provides assistance if the vehicle breaks down, including towing or on-site repairs.
Lost Keys: Helps if you lose or have your keys stolen.

Equipment Insurance:
Equipment and Accessories: Covers internal equipment like appliances, entertainment systems, and furniture if they are damaged or stolen.
Personal Belongings: Protects personal items inside the vehicle.

Non-Usage Insurance:
Off-Season Coverage: Some policies offer protection even when the vehicle is not in use, covering risks like damage from storage.
